Become a Benefactor

Our humble organisation relies on the kind support of the community. Your support will send a powerful message and improve not only the our Orginsation but also, more importantly, the community. Join us in making a difference.

Become a Benefactor

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you) you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months.

As a benefactor you will also receive:

  • Two complimentary tickets to our next concert
  • Placed on our exclusive mailing list for our newsletter
  • Invited to be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function.
